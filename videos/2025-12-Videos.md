## Avoir de l’impact en tant qu’Engineering Manager (Dimitri BAELI et Benoit GUILLON)

[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4ekJwOV45ro)

Leadership Path (Individual Contributor) vs Management Path (Engineering Manager)

"**Pendulum**" entre les 2 Career Path : importance d'alterner

### 4 failures of Engineering Managers

1. Slow Delivery and Low Quality (Operational Excellence)
2. Low Business Outcome
3. Poor Company Culture
4. No Innovation nor Surprises

Balance : assess, build and maintain an efficient team

Measure the success of the System AND of the People

### Kaizen vs Kaikaku

- Kaizen : Continuous Improvement -> Local Optimum
- Kaikaku : Break things. Radical Change -> Global Optimum

### Team Craftsmanship

Hands off -> **Indirect** impact

#### Assess the team maturity

Define a **maturity model**.

Tuckman model :
- Forming
- Storming
- Norming
- Performing

If your team was a Software ? Alpha, Beta, RC, GA, Deprecated ?

Situational Leadership.

#### Create a Culture of Result

EM -> pas de Lead par l'exemple (sauf Comportemental)

Focus on **Outcomes**, not **Outputs**

#### Individual Management and Hiring

Aspect primordial

- Help Engineers measure and optimise impact
- Grow Engineers for Team Benefit
- Next step can be outside the Team

#### Bring the company to the Team (and vice versa !)

Not a Gateway nor Proxy but a DNS : point to the right person/resource.

Ne pas protéger l'équipe, mais la connecter au reste de l'entreprise.

Présenter et évangéliser les concepts de stratégie et de revenus.
